Redeem Points from Sale order

Step 1 : Click on Redeem Points button.

Step 2 : Enter valid no. of Redeem points.

Step 3 : Respective value of Redeem points get subtracted from product price.

Redeem Points from POS

Step 1 : Select product, set customer and click on Payment.

Step 2 : Click on Redeem Loyalty button.

Step 3 : Enter valid no. of Redeem points.

Step 4 : Redeem points count and subtracted value shown on the page. Then validate payment.

Redeem Points from Website

Step 1 : Select product.

Step 2 : Click on add to cart.

Step 3 : Enter valid Redeem points in Point to use/redeem.

Step 4 : Redeem points count and subtracted value shown on the page. Then confirm payment.

Redeem point value validations

No. of Redeem points, its redemption limit should be same as stated in customer's profile and Redeem points value should not exceed product price.

Valid numeric value for redeem points should be enter by user.

How do Loyalty and Reward Points works?

Interesting way to attract customers by providing Reward points on deals. In this application, customer earns Reward points on every deal. Customer can redeem that points in his further deals to get discount on his shopping. You can customize type of customer, his reward points, redemption limit and reward applicable products etc.

How can I earn rewards?

You can earn points on every deal. Points get added in your cart automatically after each deal. On special occasion like birthday, you are gifted with some reward points.
